40 under 40

Congratulations to Wasim, the founder of Loop, for being named in the 40 under 40 list by "the marker"! This prestigious list recognizes young entrepreneurs who are making significant contributions in their respective fields. Being named on this list is no small feat, and it speaks to Wasim's hard work and dedication to building a successful business. Since 2015, Loop has worked with over 15,000 participants, investing in the next generation of tech talents. Wasim's dedication to building a successful business and nurturing young talent has earned him this recognition. We at Loop are incredibly proud of Wasim and all that he has accomplished. Congratulations again!
